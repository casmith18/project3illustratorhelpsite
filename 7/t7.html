Using the Pen Tool

Why This Matters

The Pen Tool gives you total control over your lines and shapes. Whether you're tracing a custom shape or creating a decorative accent for your spread, this tool lets you go beyond basic rectangles and circles.

Step 1: Draw a Straight Line or Shape

Select the Pen Tool (p) from the toolbar. Click once to create a point, then click somewhere else to draw a straight line between the two.

Click a few more times to create a shape like a triangle or a jagged graphic border. To close the shape, click the first point again.

Pen Tool creating a basic straight-edged shape.

Step 2: Draw a Curve

Click once to place your first point, then click and drag the next point. As you drag, you'll see two handles appear. These control the shape of the curve.

Try creating a swooping curve or a wavy divider that spans the top of your layout!

A curve being edited with Direct Selection Tool.

Step 4: Use the Pen Tool for Decorative Elements

Try using the Pen Tool to create a custom shape or accent graphic that frames your headline or follows the edge of a photo. These little touches can make your spread feel way more polished and creative!
