Welcome to Illustrator Made Easy!

This site was created to help beginners build confidence using Adobe Illustrator through a hands-on, project-based approach. Each tutorial will walk you through a tool or feature while guiding you through the process of creating a simple magazine spread. By the end, you'll know how to structure a layout, add text and graphics, and export your final design.

To get started, click on Tutorial 1. You can work through the lessons in order or revisit any topic you want to practice more.
